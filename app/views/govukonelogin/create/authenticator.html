Set up an authenticator app

  1. Open your authenticator app on your smartphone, tablet or computer.

    2. I don't have an authenticator app

    You can download an authenticator app for your smartphone or tablet from your app store.

    If you don’t have a smartphone or tablet, search online for an authenticator app for your computer.

    You can use any authenticator app.

  2. Use the authenticator app to scan the QR code.


    I cannot scan the QR code

    You can enter the secret key into your authenticator app instead.

    Secret key: QWMA HC7F SZQW YIJJ RRIT X64U KCSS OHSK

    Some authenticator apps call the secret key a ‘code’.

  3. The authenticator app will show a security code.
  4. Enter the security code.

This is the 6-digit number shown in your authenticator app
