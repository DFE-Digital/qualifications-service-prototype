About GOV.UK One Login

GOV.UK One Login is new. At the moment you can only use your GOV.UK One Login with:

  • Access your teaching qualifications
  • Apply for a vehicle operator licence
  • GOV.UK email subscriptions
  • LITE (Licensing for International Trade and Enterprise)
  • Request a basic DBS check

GOV.UK One Login is currently separate from other government accounts (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).
